Biography

I am a software developer/NLP researcher, with a Bachelor’s in CS and a Master’s in Mathematics from BITS Goa. I am currently working at Nutanix in the Overlay Networking team as a Member of Technical Staff-II. On the research front, I am interning at Carnegie Mellon University and APPCAIR, BITS Goa, with special focus on Clinical NLP. I love contributing to open source and I am one of the leading contributors to KerasNLP . I am a core member of the Language Research Group (LRG).
