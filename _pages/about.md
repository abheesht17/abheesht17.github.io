I'm Abheesht Sharma, an ML Engineer at Google working on the Keras ecosystem.
I build and maintain [KerasHub](https://github.com/keras-team/keras-hub),
[KerasRS](https://github.com/keras-team/keras-rs), and
[Tunix](https://github.com/google/tunix).

Previously, I was an Applied Scientist at Amazon, where I worked on ML techniques
for advertisement fraud detection at scale. I hold a B.E. in Computer Science from
BITS Pilani, Goa.

My research interests span NLP, recommender systems, and deep learning.
I'm passionate about open-source ML tooling and making state-of-the-art models
accessible to practitioners.
